Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of rewards can I get through Tivly?

All kinds! The rewards are completely customized by the stores, so tell your favorite places what rewards you want to see!

If I Connect through Facebook, will Tivly post anything without permission?

No way! You control exactly what you do and do not want to share.

Do I have to enter my credit card to participate?

Nope! You can take recommendations from friends and give recommendations without registering your card. However, to redeem your rewards, you must register your card.

How do you keep my information secure?

We take every step to protect your information. We don't store your credit card information on our servers, we use a service called CardSpring that protects your information with PCI compliant bank-level security.

What is the process of registering my business with tivly?

We leverage a unique technology that allows us to eliminate all hardware and software installation. Once you join the Tivly platform, we will have Tivly up and running for your business in one week! Works with all payment cards and processors.
