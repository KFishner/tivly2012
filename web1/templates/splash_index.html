Why Choose tivly to discover

  • Share your favorite stores - Send recommendations and discounts to friends through Facebook, Twitter, and email. When they take your recommendations, you get rewarded.
  • Discover your friends' great finds - Everyone has a friend who is "in the know." Now you can take their recommendations with ease (and get "in the know" yourself)!
  • Rewards automatically on your credit card - Keep swiping your card at your favorite stores and tivly automatically loads rewards onto your card - just swipe to redeem.
  • All your favorite stores in one place - Now when you recommend a store to a friend, they get the full details - offerings, location, hours - everything they need to enjoy your favorite spot!
